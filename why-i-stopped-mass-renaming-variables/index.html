Why I stopped mass-renaming variables

October 2025 · 1 min read

The best code review comment I ever received was: "I can read this, but I have to want to."

It was on a PR where I'd renamed forty variables in one pass. Every name was technically better. But the diff was unreadable, and the reviewer couldn't tell what had actually changed versus what was cosmetic.

The lesson

Renaming is refactoring. Refactoring deserves its own commit — or better, its own PR.
